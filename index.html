About Us

Douro Hotel stands on the site of a historic 19th‑century building constructed in 1877, a structure that predates the beginning of the Dom Luís I Bridge by nearly four years. This old riverside building was later acquired by the founder, Elnaz Hanifian, who recognized its rare potential due to its extraordinary location directly beneath the iconic bridge.

Transforming this heritage structure into a boutique luxury hotel required overcoming major architectural and regulatory challenges. Because part of the land lies under the bridge’s main pillar, excavation and construction were highly restricted. In fact, building directly beneath the pillar was strictly prohibited, which led to the hotel’s distinctive design: two separated volumes, with the central section intentionally left open to respect structural safety.

Despite these limitations, the project succeeded through innovative architectural solutions and persistent effort. Today, this unique configuration—combined with the hotel’s privileged position on the riverbank—gives Douro Hotel its signature character. Most rooms offer uninterrupted views of the Douro River and the Dom Luís I Bridge, making the hotel one of the most visually striking stays in Porto and Gaia.

Founder

Douro Hotel was created by Elnaz Hanifian, an individual passionate about heritage architecture, riverside landscapes, and the craft of boutique hospitality. Her vision was to transform a forgotten historic structure into a calm, elegant, and meaningful place for travelers seeking authenticity and beauty.

Sustainability

Douro Hotel is committed to a thoughtful and responsible approach to hospitality. Our sustainability philosophy is built on respect for the historic 1877 building, careful resource management, and a deep connection to the Douro River environment. We prioritize energy‑efficient systems, reduced water consumption, and local sourcing across our operations. By preserving the architectural heritage of the site and minimizing our ecological footprint, we aim to offer guests a luxury experience that is both authentic and environmentally conscious.

Origens Restaurant

Origens Restaurant – Michelin‑Level Dining by the River

Origens is the signature restaurant of Douro Hotel, offering contemporary Portuguese cuisine with international influences, crafted from seasonal, locally sourced ingredients. Our tasting menus celebrate the flavors of the Douro Valley, the Atlantic coast, and the rich culinary heritage of Porto.

Executive Chef: João Marques (A concept chef for the project — internationally trained and known for refined modern Portuguese cuisine.)

Details:
